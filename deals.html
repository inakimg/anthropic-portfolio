A Deal I Lost (And What I Learned)

Lost Regional Logistics Platform

▼
Indonesia · ML Platform · 2023 · Lost to AWS
What happened: $500K ARR opportunity. I had the technical champion (VP Engineering), strong POC results, and executive sponsor interest. Lost at the final stage to AWS.

Why I lost: I focused on the technical buyer and didn't multi-thread to procurement early enough. AWS had an existing MSA and procurement relationship. By the time I engaged their CFO on commercial terms, the "path of least resistance" was already set.

What I learned: Champion is not enough. Now I map the full buying committee in Week 1: Economic Buyer, Technical Buyer, Procurement, Legal. I ask "Who else needs to say yes?" in every discovery call. I build relationships with finance before presenting commercial terms.

How it changed me: This loss taught me that enterprise sales is multi-stakeholder navigation, not just relationship building. I now use explicit org mapping for every strategic deal.
