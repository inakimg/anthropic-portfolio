Why Anthropic

Not just a job application — this is why I care.

The Mission Resonates

Anthropic is building AI that's genuinely helpful, harmless, and honest. That's not a tagline — it's a technical commitment backed by research I actually read and trust.

What draws me in is the scientific rigor. Constitutional AI, interpretability research, the focus on understanding what's actually happening inside these models — this is frontier work that matters. And Anthropic publishes it openly, advancing the entire field.

I help customers across SEA deploy Claude in production — from fintech fraud detection to healthtech triage to enterprise knowledge systems. Seeing how Claude handles nuanced, high-stakes use cases every day only deepens my conviction that this is the right approach to AI.

I volunteer as a Master Contributor at AI Singapore because I genuinely believe responsible AI matters. This isn't something I say in interviews — it's something I do.
