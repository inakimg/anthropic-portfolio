Why Anthropic

I come from a butcher's family in Barcelona. My parents worked their whole lives so I could have opportunities like this one.

That's why I work the way I do. I may not have all the answers, but I will always find a way. I might not be the smartest in the room, but no one works harder than me.

These principles helped me close 40+ AI deployments across ASEAN. I learned why deployments fail. I experienced the local and regulatory nuances firsthand — by failing many times and getting back up.

I became a dad 13 months ago. AI safety isn't abstract anymore — it's about the world my daughter will grow up in.

Anthropic is doing the most important work in AI. Constitutional AI, interpretability research, building systems that are genuinely helpful and safe. I want to contribute to that mission. I want a seat on that bus.

I played football semi-professionally in Spain's 3rd division before tech. The discipline, the teamwork, the refusal to give up — that never left me. It just found a new arena.

There's always a way. You just need to find it.
