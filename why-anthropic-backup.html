Why Anthropic

I come from a butcher's family in Barcelona. My parents worked their whole lives so I could have opportunities like this one — to be part of the lab building the future of AI.

That's why I work the way I do. I may not have all the answers, but I will always find a way. I might not be the smartest in the room, but no one works harder than me.

My parents taught me that trust is built one honest interaction at a time.

That's what drew me to Anthropic — a company building AI the same way my family built their business: with integrity, thinking about the long term, and giving customers what they actually need, not what's easiest to sell.

I worked at the butcher shop as a kid. Selling at the counter, I learned to give customers what they wanted — not what you wanted to sell. That's exactly how Anthropic operates. No ads. No engagement tricks. Just solving your problem as fast as possible.

At the butcher shop

Never cut corners. Know every customer by name. Build trust that lasts generations.

At Anthropic

Never cut corners on safety. Constitutional AI. Build trust that lasts generations.

These principles helped me guide 40+ digital natives and startups to embed AI in ways that drive the outcomes they actually care about. I learned why deployments fail. I experienced ASEAN's regulatory nuances firsthand — by failing many times and getting back up.

I became a dad 13 months ago. AI safety isn't abstract anymore. It's about the world my daughter will grow up in.
